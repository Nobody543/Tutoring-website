Oxford's Current Standard
02 — Specialism
Oxbridge Interview Coaching

Oxford and Cambridge interviews are designed to probe the way you think, not simply what you know. I prepare you to approach unseen problems clearly and confidently — to articulate your reasoning under pressure, to engage constructively with deliberate challenge, and to demonstrate the genuine mathematical curiosity these institutions want to see. The Programme

The five-stage plan

Every tutee follows the same five-stage structure, adapted to their individual starting point and timeline. The programme is designed so that each stage genuinely builds on the last — there are no shortcuts, but there are no wasted sessions either.

The goal at every step is not just performance on the test, but a genuine shift in how you approach mathematical problems. That's what separates candidates who score well from those who plateau.

We begin by working out exactly where you are. The free 30-minute introductory session is the right place to do that — I'll get a clear picture of your current level and give you an honest assessment of what the programme will look like for you specifically.

01
Consolidate the foundations
Ensure your A-Level knowledge is genuinely secure — not just familiar. TMUA does not test advanced content, but it demands that core material is understood with real precision. We identify and close any gaps before building on top of them.
02
Develop the problem-solving toolkit
TMUA rewards a specific kind of mathematical reasoning that goes well beyond standard A-Level technique. We build the skills of logical deduction, lateral thinking, and efficient problem-framing that the test is explicitly designed to assess.
03
Master the TMUA
Apply the method under timed, realistic conditions. We work through the full range of question types, refine your approach to time management, and address the patterns of error that most commonly cost candidates marks — including the ones that aren't obvious until it's too late.
04
Prepare for interview
Move from written test to live problem-solving. We focus on thinking out loud, responding constructively to challenge, and demonstrating the genuine mathematical curiosity that distinguishes strong interview candidates.
05
Secure the place
Arrive at every stage of the application process prepared, confident, and with a precise understanding of what you're being assessed on. The whole programme is built around this outcome from the very first session.
